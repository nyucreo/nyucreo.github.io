The NYU Center for Robotics and Embodied Intelligence (CREO) seeks applications for a **full-time, non-tenure-track Faculty Fellow** in Robotics and Embodied AI. This is a **one-year appointment**, **renewable** based on satisfactory performance, and is well suited for promising researchers in robotics or embodied AI across theory, computation, hardware, and real-world applications. The Fellow will receive a competitive salary, full NYU benefits, and support for pursuing independent research and cross-disciplinary collaboration within CREO.

Review of applications will begin **January 01, 2026** and will continue until the position is filled. Anticipated start date is **Summer 2026** (flexible).
